Information & Contact

Dear User, Welcome to this Accessible Navigation Map. This website was created within a semester project and aims to offer a navigation for regular & physically disabled people in the city of Zurich.
Following, a detailed description of how to proceed is scripted.


Step-by-Step Instruction:

1. Permission of position

Allow the website to access your position.

2. Enter destination

Put your suggested destination into the box telling "Enter destination...".

3. Choice of Mobility

Choose whether the navigation should be applied for physically disabled people and therefore avoid steep streets.

4. Start navigation

The navigation can be started by pressing the play-Button. A suitable route should be suggested within a few seconds.

>

5. Obstacles

Whenever an obstacle occurs on your trip, please mark it by pressing the triangular sign. You can choose the severity of the obstacle (1-5).

>

6. Stop navigation

To stop the navigation, press the pause-Button. In case of arriving at the suggested destination, you will get a message and the navigation will stop automatically.
